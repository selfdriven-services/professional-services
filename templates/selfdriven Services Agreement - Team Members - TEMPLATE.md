**2\. Background**

selfdriven operates a professional services organisation available at [https://selfdriven.services](https://selfdriven.services).

The Team Member provides professional services in, orchestration, conducting and advisory capacities.

The Parties wish to establish the terms under which the Team Member provides services and participates in revenue sharing and SDS token recognition.

## **3\. Nature of Relationship**

The Team Member is engaged as an independent contractor.

Nothing in this Agreement creates employment, partnership, joint venture or agency.

The Team Member has no authority to bind selfdriven unless expressly authorised in writing.

The Team Member is responsible for all tax, superannuation, insurance and statutory obligations.

## **4\. Scope of Services**

The Team Member will provide professional services including knowledge, orchestration and conducting services across nominated Areas of Focus as referenced at:

[https://actuate.selfdriven.community/framework/areas-of-focus](https://actuate.selfdriven.community/framework/areas-of-focus)

Specific customer engagements may be governed by project-specific agreements, statements of work (SOWs), or customer contracts, which operate in conjunction with this Agreement.

The Team Member agrees to maintain clear lines of communication and act in good faith in all engagements.

## **5\. Revenue Sharing & Payment**

Revenue Share:

For receipted project payments from customers attributable to the Team Member’s services:

* 90% to the Team Member  
* 10% retained by selfdriven

Of the 10% retained by selfdriven, 5% of total service revenue is distributed to SDS token holders in accordance with the SDS framework.

Invoicing:

The Team Member must submit invoices monthly.

Payment Terms:

Payments will be made:

* In AUD  
* Within fourteen (14) days of month end  
* To the bank account nominated in the Agreement Data section  
* Subject to receipt of cleared customer funds unless otherwise agreed in writing.

GST:

If GST applies, it must be clearly identified on invoices.

## **6\. SDS Token Recognition**

A total of 100,000 SDS tokens exist for original selfdriven Services team members.

20,000 SDS tokens have been allocated to the originating selfdriven team member (Mark Byers).

10,000 SDS tokens are allocated to you as an original team member after 2 months as a team member.

SDS tokens:

* Do not represent equity or shares in selfdriven Pty Ltd.  
* Do not confer ownership rights in the company.  
* Provide governance participation within the services organisation framework.  
* Participate in distribution of 5% of total service revenue, in proportion to SDS holdings based on the amount of SDS tokens allocated in total.

Token mechanics, governance rights and distribution models may be further defined in an SDS framework document.

## **7\. Intellectual Property**

Customer Deliverables:

Unless otherwise agreed, intellectual property created specifically for a customer engagement is owned by the customer upon payment.

Pre-Existing IP:

Each Party retains ownership of intellectual property developed independently prior to this Agreement.

selfdriven Services Resources:

Methods, templates, protocols, branding, and internal frameworks remain the property of selfdriven Services unless otherwise agreed.

## **8\. Confidentiality**

The Team Member must keep confidential all information relating to:

* selfdriven operations  
* associated projects

## **10\. Non-Solicitation**

During the term of this Agreement and for twelve (12) months after termination, the Team Member must not:

Without written consent from selfdriven Services.

Liability is limited to the total fees received by the Team Member in the twelve (12) months preceding a claim.

Outstanding payments remain payable upon termination.

SDS tokens treatment upon termination will be governed by the SDS framework.
